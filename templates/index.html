Welcome to the Physics & Chemistry Simulator

This interactive web app allows you to explore and visualize fundamental topics across physics and chemistry. Choose a module below to begin:

  • Thermodynamics – Explore laws of energy, entropy, and simulate heat engines.
  • Quantum Mechanics – Solve Schrödinger's equation and view quantum wavefunctions.
  • General Relativity – Compute Schwarzschild radii and explore gravity's effect on spacetime.
  • Cosmology – Understand the expanding universe through Hubble’s Law and scale factors.

Each module includes major equations, interactive charts, and real calculations to help you learn how the universe works from atoms to galaxies.
